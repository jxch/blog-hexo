Hypothesis:
$h_\theta(x)=\theta_0+\theta_1x$

Parameters:
$\theta_0, \theta_1$

Cost Function:
$J(\theta_0,\theta_1)=\frac{1}{2m} \sum_{i=0}^m (h_\theta(x^{(i)})-y^{(i)})^2$

Goal:
$minimize_{\theta_0,\theta_1}J(\theta_0,\theta_1)$
